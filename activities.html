Cat Activities

In Purr City, cats are intelligent and curious. Most importantly, cats like to have fun!

Animal chess

Hmmmmm, how to take the Lion King? Animal chess is a game with different animal pieces which move in unique directions. The aim is to attack the other player's King so he cannot escape.

Altruism

Some cats are not so fortunate to have a loving home. Raise money and bake fishcakes for homeless cats. Altruism is thinking about the well-being of others, it comes from the Latin word alter meaning other. This is also where we get the word alternative from, meaning another option or possibiity.

Bootcamp

Get fit at bootcamp! Bootcamp is intensive training over a short period of time.

Cat cafe


Have a drink, relax and get to know other cats.

Church

Is there more to life than this world?

Come in and discover if there is cat heaven.

Motor-racing

Race other cats on tracks through created desert, mountain and coastal terrains. A terrain is a piece of land with regards to its physical features. This comes from the Latin word terra meaning land/earth, and it is also where we get the word territory from.

Languages

Learn Spanish, Mandarin and more at a language school: Cat Academy

Limbo

Can you do the limbo dance? Practising the limbo improves your flexibility.

Rock-climbing

Cats are active and like the outdoors.

Karate & self-defence

Ka-pow! Learn to defend yourself against dogs before running up a tree.

Singing

Learn to serenade other cats. Breathe in properly and project your voice.

Tree climbing

Explore different trees. Have adventures with fellow cats - team bonding!
